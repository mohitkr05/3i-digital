You are a senior frontend engineer and UX designer specialising in calm, trust-first B2B websites for Australian tech founders.

Your task is to build a single, self-contained HTML page for a boutique digital agency called “3I Digital”.

GOAL
The website’s purpose is NOT marketing or SEO.
Its purpose is to build trust and make founders feel safe booking a short call.

TARGET AUDIENCE
- Tech startup founders
- Small to medium business owners
- Australian context
- Time-poor, tool-fatigued, sceptical of marketing hype

TONE
- Calm
- Clear
- Confident
- Plain English
- No buzzwords
- No hype
- No growth promises

DESIGN PRINCIPLES
- Minimalist
- White or very light background
- Neutral colour palette (grey, charcoal, muted blue)
- Generous whitespace
- Simple typography
- No animations
- No gradients
- No stock “growth” imagery
- No icons unless truly necessary

TECH REQUIREMENTS
- Generate ONE HTML file only
- Use semantic HTML5
- Use Tailwind CSS via CDN
- No JavaScript frameworks
- Minimal or no JavaScript (only if absolutely required)
- Mobile responsive
- Fast loading
- Clean, readable markup

PAGE STRUCTURE
All sections must exist on the same page, in this order:

1. Header / Hero
2. Problems We Solve
3. What We Manage
4. Who It’s For / Who It’s Not For
5. How It Works
6. About 3I Digital
7. Services (Included / Not Included)
8. Contact
9. Footer with legal links (Privacy Policy, Terms – placeholders)

CONTENT REQUIREMENTS

HERO SECTION
Headline:
"Managed Digital Presence for Tech Founders"

Subheadline:
"We manage your website and social presence so you can focus on growing the business, not managing tools."

Primary CTA:
"Book a 20-minute chat"

PROBLEMS SECTION
List 3 short, founder-relevant problems:
- Digital presence feels neglected
- Too many tools to manage
- No single point of accountability

WHAT WE MANAGE
Plain list:
- Website monitoring and updates
- Social presence management
- Content scheduling
- Monthly review and reporting
- One accountable point of contact

WHO IT’S FOR / NOT FOR
Two columns:
GOOD FIT IF YOU:
- Are a founder or small team
- Want things handled quietly
- Don’t want to manage tools

NOT A GOOD FIT IF YOU:
- Want daily posting
- Expect growth guarantees
- Enjoy managing tools yourself

HOW IT WORKS
Simple 3 steps:
1. Short discovery call
2. We take over management
3. Monthly check-in and adjustments

ABOUT SECTION
Founder-focused explanation:
- Why 3I Digital exists
- Emphasis on being small, hands-on, accountable
- No fake team or corporate language

SERVICES SECTION
Clearly separated lists:

INCLUDED:
- Website uptime and updates
- Social presence management
- Content scheduling
- Monthly review and summary
- Single point of contact

NOT INCLUDED:
- Paid advertising
- SEO growth guarantees
- Daily posting
- Lead generation promises

CONTACT SECTION
- Short reassuring intro
- Simple contact form (name, email, message)
- Placeholder link for calendar booking
- No pressure language

FOOTER
- Minimal
- Business name
- Placeholder links to Privacy Policy and Terms

COPY RULES
- Short sentences
- Short paragraphs
- Plain Australian English
- Avoid words like “leverage”, “synergy”, “scale”, “growth hacking”
- Prefer clarity over persuasion

OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS
- Output ONLY the complete HTML file
- Include Tailwind CDN link in <head>
- Use clean, semantic HTML
- Use Tailwind utility classes sparingly
- No additional explanations or commentary

FINAL CHECK
The page should make a founder think:
“Okay, this feels safe, simple, and sensible.”

If anything feels like marketing, remove it.
If anything adds complexity, remove it.
If anything doesn’t reduce doubt, remove it.
